Artistic Journey

  • Dhulikhel
  • 2016
  • 14

I had always felt a pull towards art, but it wasn't until I was going through a difficult time in my life that I decided to take it seriously. I had always admired the works of famous artists and dreamed of creating something just as beautiful.

I started by taking some basic drawing classes and practicing every day. I found that the more I created, the more I wanted to learn and grow as an artist. I also started attending local art events and connecting with other artists, who offered guidance and encouragement.

As I continued to develop my skills and create new pieces, I realized that art was not just a hobby, but a true passion. I set a goal for myself to participate in a group art show within the next six months, and I worked tirelessly to achieve it.

"Passion is the fire that lights up the soul and drives us to achieve the impossible."

Looking back on my journey, I realized that taking the leap and following my passion for art was one of the best decisions I ever made. It has brought me joy, purpose, and a sense of fulfillment that I never imagined possible.
